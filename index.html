A Little Bit About Me

I grew up in Syracuse, NY. I went to grade school there, and feeling quite comfortable with the area, I decided to go to college there. I ended up getting a degree in engineering. I learned a lot during those four years, but after graduating and doing a little soul searching, I decided that the career path I had chosen wasn't right for me. I started looking into programming while I was searching for a job, and around the time I finished my first project, I knew that it was something I could do for the rest of my life. I've come to Epicodus in the hopes that I can start a career as a programmer, and it already feels like one of the best decisions I've ever made.

Why Programming?

I graduated college with a degree in engineering. After I finished up, I started applying for jobs, had several interviews, but had no luck getting hired. Eventually some graduates of the school I went to offered me some consulting work for their startup company. I worked with them for a little while, but I realized that it just wasn't the kind of work I wanted to do. I started looking for avenues into other career paths, and eventually found coding. I learned a bit on my own and made a couple of projects, and found that it was right up my alley. I'm currently learning how to program at Epicodus, and am loving every minute of it!
